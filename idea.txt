Phase 1 — Engine
Collisions, movement, combat, enemies, sound, map, visuals. This is the foundation — making sure everything works mechanically.

Phase 2 — Systems 
Soul/XP system (enemies drop souls, counter on screen)
Death and respawn (lose souls, respawn at bonfire)
Wave spawner (timer, increasing difficulty each wave)
Health system (player HP bar, enemy HP, damage numbers)
Inventory and items (weapon upgrades, abilities)
NPC interaction (walk up, press E, open shop/dialog)
Save system (bank souls, persist between sessions)

Phase 3 — Content 
Story and dialogs
Multiple enemy types with different behaviors
Boss fights with patterns
Multiple maps/areas
Progression and unlocks
Balancing (how many souls per enemy, item costs, wave difficulty curve)
Menu screens, pause, game over
